Our story

WELCOME to The Cookies Shop!


We are a family-owned bakery located in the heart of
Kegalle,Sri lanka, dedicated to providing our
customers with the highest quality ingredients,
balanced flavors, and unique styles.
We are passionate about using simple, natural
ingredients to create delicious treats that we are sure
you will enjoy.
Our attention to detail ensures that each treat is a
special moment. We began baking in 2018 and are
excited to share our passion for excellence with our
community.
